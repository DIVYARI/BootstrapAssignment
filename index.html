Most Popular Modern Paintings

Buyers of modern paintings should know the three most popular modern painting styles and how to distinguish one from the other.

Abstract Art

Abstract expressionism paintings are emotionally intense and spontaneously created by the artist. It is often synonymous with action or gesture paintings that have an anarchic and rebellious feel.

Surrealism

Surrealism is a modern painting style that juxtaposes various images together to give a startling effect. The images in these paintings are often illogical and have a dream-like quality about them.
